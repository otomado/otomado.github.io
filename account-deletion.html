Otomado Account Deletion & Data Retention Information

At Otomado, we value your privacy and provide you with full control over your personal data. If you wish to delete your Otomado account and request the removal of your associated data, please review the following information and follow the instructions below.

How to Delete Your Account

  1. Log In: Sign in to your Otomado account using your registered email address or Google account.
  2. Navigate to Account Settings: Once logged in, go to the account settings menu within the app.
  3. Initiate Account Deletion: Click the "Delete Account" option and follow the on-screen instructions to confirm your request.
  4. Confirmation: After you confirm, your account deletion request will be processed immediately. If you require assistance, please contact our support team at support@otomado.com.

Data Deletion and Retention Details

When you delete your account, the following actions will be taken:

Immediate Deletion:

  • Authentication and Profile Information: Your email address and profile data—including country code and any self-introduction text—will be deleted immediately.
  • Audio Data: Audio recordings are automatically deleted within one day after streaming. However, if the audio is reported as inappropriate, the data will be retained for investigation purposes for 90 days before deletion.

Data That May Be Retained:

  • Service Integrity & Legal Obligations: Some records, such as reports of inappropriate audio, account suspension information, and inquiry histories, may be retained for up to one year (or as required by applicable laws) to ensure community safety, service integrity, and compliance with legal obligations.
  • Analytics and Crash Data: Non-personally identifiable usage data (e.g., from Firebase Analytics or Crashlytics) may be retained in accordance with our data retention policies.

Important Note

By proceeding with the account deletion process, you acknowledge that this action is irreversible. Once your account and associated data are deleted, you will lose access to the Otomado service, and recovery of deleted data will not be possible.

If you have any questions or need further assistance regarding account deletion, please contact us at support@otomado.com.
